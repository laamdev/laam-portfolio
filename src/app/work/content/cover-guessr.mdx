Cover Guessr is a music discovery game that challenges players to identify album covers across different genres and eras. The project was inspired by the art of album cover design and how it reflects musical evolution over time.

The game features multiple play modes:

- Classic Mode: Guess the release year of random album covers
- Genre Challenge: Identify the musical genre based on cover art
- Artist Mode: Match covers to correct artists
- Daily Challenge: Compete with other players on a curated daily selection

Technical features include:

- Integration with Spotify's API for comprehensive album data
- Real-time leaderboards using Supabase
- Progressive Web App capabilities for offline play
- Social sharing of results and achievements
- Personal statistics tracking and achievements system

The user interface was designed to be both playful and intuitive, with smooth animations and transitions that enhance the gaming experience. Special attention was paid to image optimization to ensure quick loading times while maintaining cover art quality.

The game has become a fun way for music enthusiasts to test their knowledge while discovering new artists and albums. Future updates will include multiplayer modes, custom playlists creation, and integration with more music streaming services.
